Gensho de wa omo ni eigo o taishō to shi ta shizen gengo shori o toriatsukatte i masu. Naiyō ya kangaekata no ōku wa gengo ni izon shi nai mono de wa ari masu ga, tango no wakachigaki o shi nai ten ya tōgo kōzō tō no chigai kara, nihongo o taishō to suru baai, ikutsu ka ki o tsuke nakere ba ike nai ten ga ari masu. Nihongo o atsukau baai ni mo ōyō dekiru yō ni, yakusha no hitori de aru hagihara masato ga, nippon muke ni gensho ni wa nai 12 shō o kakioroshi mashi ta. Koko de wa, nihongo o dono yō ni shori suru ka toyuu ten ni kuwae te, sarani manabu tame ni, nihongo no shori nikansuru, moshikuwa nihongo de kakare ta sankō bunken o habahiroku shōkai shi te i masu.
